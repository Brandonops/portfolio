About

I'm a current student who is set to graduate in May 2021 from DigitalCrafts, a bootcamp focusing on languages such as JavaScript, React.js, Python, and more. I became passionate about software development during my college career where I took an intro to HTML/CSS elective course. After graduating, I learned more about coding bootcamps which lead me to DigitalCrafts where I'm learning the skills to become a software developer.

Full Stack Software Developer.

Becoming a Software Developer has been the most exciting challenge I've taken on in my life. I've enjoyed everything from fetching API's in the front end to creating databases in the back end. However, the front end has become a strong passion of mine as I deeply enjoy connecting with the user experience of web applications.

Prior to becoming a Software Developer I was a full time student in college. During my college career I worked as a server where I learned many valuable skills that will stay with me forever. The way I connected with an audience as a server has also impacted the way I see users in web development. It's important for me to develop that connection where I know I can provide an amazing experience for my audience.
