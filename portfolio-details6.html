Ticket Senpai

Member of a 4-person development team building a centralized hub to compare ticket prices from three major ticket vendors. Responsibilities included designing and creating a search function to the application where users can easily search by a performer or team in a city and searching events happening in that city. In addition, implement the search by performer page which is fully responsive and shows results when a user searches by a performer.

Skills and Languages used in this project:
